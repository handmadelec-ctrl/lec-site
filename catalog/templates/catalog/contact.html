Quick FAQ

Do you restock sold-out bags?

Some pieces are limited. If an item is sold out, message us on WhatsApp and we’ll confirm availability or similar options.

Are measurements exact?

Measurements are approximate. Small variations and subtle handmade details are part of the artisan character of each piece and should not be considered defects.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery time depends on your location and shipping carrier. We’ll confirm the estimated delivery window after you share your shipping city and state.
